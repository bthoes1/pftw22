clouds

For this sketch, I researched Perlin Noise--something I hadn't attempted to use before. One of my goals was to let the user's approach influence what they see. Mouse position changes the x-coordinate and y-coordinate value in noise space, outputting a visual that varies widely in its definition and gives the illusion of zooming in or out on a cloudscape.
