Let's talk about llamas vs. alpacas

While llamas and alpacas may appear similar, they are actually quite different. Here are some of the things they have in common as well as things that make each unique.

  • While they may look similar, llamas usually weigh 300-450 lbs. while alpacas weigh 100-200 lbs.
  • Llamas are also taller than alpacas, standing 5.5-6.5 feet tall, while alpacas stand at 4-5 feet tall in general.
  • Both llamas and alpacas spit far less than their reputation suggests. Often this is to diffuse conflict that would otherwise lead to fighting.
  • Both product fiber that are commonly used in textiles.
